ACTIVITIES

Tell about the activities performed by team in webpage

DAY 1

Python turle - We were introduced to the world of digital patterns and colors using turtle.We asked to unleash our creative side of us by creating random patterns and we were challenged to do an animation.We went from zero graphic knowledge to a very comfortable zone where we were able to create any pattern using different colours.

DAY 2

File Handling - Karthik had come up with a very engaging session on python.We were briefed about certain functions like list and dictionaries and we were given a task.Our task was to create a shopping list using all the functions that was briefed in the morning.

DAY 3

last day for Python learning - It was another python session from Karthik in which he dealt about file handling.Karthik had made the session more interesting by throwing us 6 challenge problems of which we had to do three.Our team had the rage to complete all six but we had completed five of them.We did a contact directory, rock paper scissor game, quiz, xo game and hangman.

DAY 4

UI & UX - The most fun filled session which had mimicked our kinder-garden days at many instances was handled by Mr. Navaneeth from Alchi designs, Bangalore.He was an UI/UX expert, who began his session by making us scribble.The session demanded a lot of our artistic skills as he was trying to pull out the last shreds of creativity that still existed in some corners of our brains.He walked us through what UI/UX was and how it is done.He told us few trips and tricks to make our designs stand out.In the evening, all of collaborated to come up with a problem statement and each of the teams were asked to come up with their own unique solutions and present our ideas to the crowd.

DAY 5

UI & UX activities - The next day of UI/UX session was also equally fun.We were taught to create wire frame for an app and we were asked to design one on our own and another one for the app that our team had decided to build.In the afternoon, Navaneeth had handled the last topic which was product service in a funnily ingenious manner.He gave us play dough and had asked us to create the same for any product or a service using clay.He ended the day with a feedback session.
